# What are SparseZoo models?
SparseZoo models are models that have gone a rigorous process to ensure that they recover most of the accuracy after pruning and quantization. These models are hosted on sparsezoo.com. The models are ready for use with DeepSparse, saving you the hassle of having to optimize common models. 

![SparseZoo LLM Screenshot](/img/sparsezoo-llm-screenshot.png)

Examples of interesting SparseZoo LLMs include:
* [llama2-7b-llama2_chat_llama2_pretrain-base_quantized](https://sparsezoo.neuralmagic.com/models/llama2-7b-llama2_chat_llama2_pretrain-base_quantized?hardware=deepsparse-c6i.12xlarge&comparison=llama2-7b-llama2_chat_llama2_pretrain-base)
* [mistral-7b-open_platypus_orca_mistral_pretrain-base_quantized](https://sparsezoo.neuralmagic.com/models/mistral-7b-open_platypus_orca_mistral_pretrain-base_quantized?hardware=deepsparse-c6i.12xlarge&comparison=mistral-7b-open_platypus_orca_mistral_pretrain-base)
* [codellama-7b-evolcodealpaca_codellama_pretrain-pruned60_quantized](https://sparsezoo.neuralmagic.com/models/codellama-7b-evolcodealpaca_codellama_pretrain-pruned60_quantized?hardware=deepsparse-c6i.12xlarge&comparison=codellama-7b-evolcodealpaca_codellama_pretrain-base)
